How it works

Three minutes. One complete entry.

Step 1

Record

Talk through your clinical experience — on the bus, between clinics, wherever works. Just like telling a colleague about your day.

Step 2

Review

MediPortal structures your entry with specialty-specific reflections and maps it to your curriculum capabilities. Review it, adjust anything, confirm.

Step 3

Track

Your coverage dashboard updates automatically. See which capabilities are evidenced and where gaps remain — months before your ARCP, not days.

Features

Built for how trainees actually work.

Not another clunky admin system. A tool that respects your time.

Voice-first capture

Speak about your case. We'll handle the rest. Speaking is 3-5x faster than typing and captures the clinical nuance that text entries miss.

Curriculum mapping

Every entry automatically tagged to your curriculum capabilities. No more guessing which domains you've covered — or which you're missing.

Coverage dashboard

See exactly where your portfolio stands. Domain-by-domain breakdown with entry counts so you know what to focus on before your next ES meeting.

PDP goals that matter

Actionable development goals generated from your real clinical experiences — with deadlines, action items, and evidence requirements. Not copy-pasted templates.

Privacy by default

Patient details are automatically redacted before AI processing. Audio recordings deleted within 24 hours. UK GDPR compliant. Your patients' data never reaches the AI — only cleaned, de-identified transcripts are ever processed.
