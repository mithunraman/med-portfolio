Questions trainees ask us

Does LOGDit replace my eportfolio?
No. LOGDit drafts the entry so you don't start from a blank box — export it as a PDF and paste the structured output into whichever eportfolio your deanery requires (Trainee ePortfolio, FourteenFish, or any text field).
Which specialties do you support?
In private beta today: General Practice (RCGP) only. Internal Medicine (JRCPTB), Psychiatry (RCPsych), Emergency Medicine and Paediatrics are on the roadmap. Email us with your specialty and we'll prioritise based on demand.
How does the PII redaction actually work?
Your audio is transcribed, then a redaction pass removes patient identifiers — names, DOBs, NHS numbers, addresses, postcodes — before the transcript reaches any language model. Only the redacted text is used to generate your reflections, capability tags and PDP suggestions. Full technical detail is in our DPIA.
What about my recordings — where do they go?
Audio is stored encrypted in UK-region cloud infrastructure and automatically deleted 48 hours after transcription. You can also delete any recording manually from within the app at any time, and full account deletion removes all your content.
Is it free?
Free for the entire private beta. After launch: £49/year — and locked-in at that price for beta users, even if we raise it later.
Will I still own and write my own reflections?
Yes. LOGDit drafts structured reflections from what you said, but every section is editable and version-historied. You sign off on what goes into your portfolio — same as you would today.
Who built this?
A small team of UK doctors and engineers. We can't fix everything wrong with portfolio admin, but we can fix the blank-page problem. Reach us at hello@logdit.com.
