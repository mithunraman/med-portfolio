Sub-processors

Last updated: 17 May 2026

This page lists the third-party service providers that LOGDit uses to operate the Service ("sub-processors"). It supplements section 8 of our Privacy Policy. We will give reasonable advance notice on this page of material changes — such as adding a new sub-processor, removing one, or changing the role of an existing one — before they take effect. Where practicable we will also notify active users by email or in-app.

Each sub-processor below is bound by a written data processing agreement that restricts use of your personal data to the purposes described. In most cases the agreement is auto-incorporated by reference into the provider's terms of service that we accepted at signup. Our sub-processors are contractually prohibited from using your content to train AI or machine learning models.

Hosting and infrastructure

Akamai Technologies, Inc. (Linode) — compute

Role: Cloud compute and application hosting.
Personal data processed: All data that passes through our application servers, including account details, audio uploads, transcripts and generated reflections (in transit and during processing).
Region: United Kingdom.
Transfer mechanism: None required — processing in the UK.
Privacy and DPA: linode.com/legal-privacy.

Akamai Technologies, Inc. (Linode Object Storage)

Role: Encrypted object storage for audio recordings and exported PDFs.
Personal data processed: Audio recordings you make in the app (retained encrypted and automatically deleted within 72 hours of transcription) and PDFs you export from your entries.
Region: United Kingdom.
Transfer mechanism: None required — processing in the UK.
Privacy and DPA: linode.com/legal-privacy.

MongoDB, Inc. (MongoDB Atlas)

Role: Primary managed database for account data, transcripts, reflections, capability tags, PDP goals and version history.
Personal data processed: Account details (email, display name, specialty, training stage), redacted transcripts and AI-generated content you create in the app.
Region: Ireland — EU.
Transfer mechanism: None required — UK→EEA adequacy.
Privacy and DPA: mongodb.com/legal/privacy-policy.

AI and transcription

AssemblyAI, Inc.

Role: Speech-to-text transcription of voice notes.
Personal data processed: Audio recordings you make in the app, processed transiently and not retained by AssemblyAI under their no-storage configuration.
Region: Ireland — EU.
Transfer mechanism: None required — UK→EEA adequacy.
Privacy and DPA: assemblyai.com/legal/privacy-policy.

OpenAI, L.L.C.

Role: Large-language-model processing for transcript cleaning, redaction of unstructured identifiers, drafting reflections and tagging capabilities against the relevant curriculum.
Personal data processed: Transcripts (with structured identifiers already removed prior to this step) and prompts derived from them. Content is not used to train OpenAI's models under the standard API terms.
Region: United States.
Transfer mechanism: EU Standard Contractual Clauses with the UK Addendum, as incorporated into OpenAI's standard Data Processing Addendum. Supplementary safeguards include pre-transfer redaction of patient identifiers (Privacy Policy section 6).
Privacy and DPA: openai.com/policies/privacy-policy.

Operations and communications

Google LLC / Google Ireland Limited (Google Workspace)

Role: Two related roles. First, business email and mailbox services for the logdit.app domain (the inboxes you may correspond with us at, such as support@logdit.app and privacy@logdit.app). Second, outbound transactional email delivery via the Google Workspace SMTP relay — used to send one-time passcode (OTP) sign-in codes, account notifications and other service emails to you.
Personal data processed: Your email address (used to deliver sign-in codes and service emails); the contents of any email correspondence you initiate with us; the contents of any service email we send you, including time-limited OTP codes.
Region: EU data regions under Google Workspace data residency controls.
Transfer mechanism: EU Standard Contractual Clauses with the UK Addendum, as incorporated into the Google Workspace Customer Data Processing Addendum (Google Workspace also offers EEA-region storage controls that we apply where available).
Privacy and DPA: workspace.google.com/terms/dpa_terms.

Functional Software, Inc. (Sentry)

Role: Application error monitoring and crash diagnostics.
Personal data processed: Crash reports, error stack traces and limited technical context (app version, device type, operating system). Configured to suppress default personal data collection: we do not send your name, email, content or authentication tokens to Sentry.
Region: Germany — EU (Sentry EU data residency).
Transfer mechanism: EU Standard Contractual Clauses with the UK Addendum, as incorporated into Sentry's Data Processing Addendum.
Privacy and DPA: sentry.io/privacy.

How to be notified of changes

The simplest way to be notified is to bookmark this page and re-check the "Last updated" date above, or to subscribe to product update emails when you create your account. We will publish material changes here at least 30 days before they take effect for new sub-processors, and as soon as practicable for changes prompted by a security or operational incident.
