## Context: What the Market Scan Told Us

Before diving into the plan, here's what shapes every decision below:

- **14,865 GP trainees** as of December 2024, growing to ~20,000 by 2032 (NHS Long Term Workforce Plan)
- **61% of trainees** are at moderate or high burnout risk (GMC NTS 2025). Portfolio is a top admin complaint
- **60%+ of GP trainees already use ChatGPT** for reflections informally (Learner+ pilot data) — demand is proven
- **RCGP explicitly permits AI** in portfolios: using AI for reflection prompts, capability suggestions, and drafting is allowed. Fabricating encounters or cut-and-paste without genuine reflection is not
- **FourteenFish has a mandated monopoly** via RCGP contract (~£963/trainee over 3 years). User satisfaction is declining post-acquisition by EMIS/UnitedHealth. No AI features
- **One direct AI competitor:** Learner+ (CMEfy) — 24-person pilot with Health Innovation KSS backing, 77% found reflections easier. Pre-scale
- **ICO enforcement up 7x** in H1 2025. Edtech-specific codes in development. Compliance is existential, not optional

### Recommendation: GP Trainees (RCGP pathway)

**Why them first:**

1. **Largest addressable cohort** — 14,865 trainees today, ~20,000 by 2032
2. **Highest portfolio pain, quantified** — RCGP requires evidence across 13 capability domains. Trainees "informally report seeking experiences of little educational value to satisfy ARCP requirements" (NHS England ARCP review)
3. **Proven AI demand** — 60%+ already use ChatGPT for reflections. They want this — they just don't have a structured tool
4. **RCGP has opened the door** — official guidance permits AI-assisted reflection, which legitimises your product
5. **Strongest word-of-mouth dynamics** — VTS groups of 10-20 trainees. One advocate converts the whole group. Regional Facebook/WhatsApp groups amplify nationally
6. **Incumbent vulnerability** — FourteenFish is mandated but resented. "Gone downhill since EMIS acquisition." £963 cost called a "monopoly fee" in a public letter to the Health Secretary
7. **Curriculum fully built** — GP specialty config (capabilities, entry types, templates, training stages) is complete and ready

### Positioning Statement

> For GP trainees who spend their evenings writing portfolio entries, **Portfolio** is an AI-powered reflection assistant that turns your clinical experiences into structured, capability-mapped entries — just by talking about your day. Unlike using ChatGPT directly, Portfolio knows the RCGP curriculum and tracks your coverage gaps. Unlike FourteenFish, it actually helps you create better entries, not just store them.

### Why Not Just Use ChatGPT? (Key Differentiators)

This is the most important competitive question — 60% of your target users already have a free alternative.

1. **Curriculum intelligence** — Portfolio knows all 13 RCGP capability domains and maps entries to specific capabilities with confidence scores. ChatGPT doesn't know what capabilities exist, let alone which ones you're missing
2. **Coverage dashboard** — See exactly which capabilities are evidenced and which have gaps, by review period. ChatGPT gives you an entry; Portfolio gives you a portfolio strategy
3. **Voice-first workflow** — Talk about your clinical experience on the bus home. Portfolio transcribes, redacts PII, structures, and maps it. ChatGPT requires you to type a prompt and structure the output yourself
4. **Structured follow-ups** — Portfolio asks targeted questions to fill gaps in your narrative ("What was your differential diagnosis?", "How did this change your practice?"). ChatGPT gives you what you asked for, nothing more
5. **PDP goal generation** — Automatically suggests professional development goals based on your reflections. ChatGPT doesn't track your development trajectory
6. **RCGP-aligned design** — Built to follow RCGP guidance on AI use: real experiences, genuine reflection, human review. Not a copy-paste machine

## Messaging

### Key Messages by Audience

| Audience | Primary Message | Proof Point | Objection to Pre-empt |
|----------|----------------|-------------|----------------------|
| **GP trainee (time-poor)** | "Talk about your day. Get a portfolio entry. Get your evenings back." | Demo: 2-minute voice note → complete structured reflection with capabilities tagged | "I don't have time to learn a new tool" → onboarding takes 60 seconds |
| **GP trainee (ARCP-anxious)** | "See exactly which capabilities you're missing — before your supervisor does." | Coverage dashboard screenshot showing gap analysis by domain | "Will this actually help me pass ARCP?" → show gap-to-action workflow |
| **GP trainee (sceptical about AI)** | "You still own every word. | **GP trainee (privacy-conscious)** | "Your clinical narratives are yours. Audio is auto-redacted for PII. We never train on your data." | PII redaction demo, link to privacy policy | "Is my patient data safe?" → explain redaction, data residency, no model training |
| **Training programme director** | "Your trainees produce better reflections and spend less time on admin. Portfolio follows RCGP AI guidance." | Side-by-side: manual entry vs. Portfolio-assisted entry quality comparison | "Is this just AI-generated rubbish?" → show human-in-the-loop, RCGP alignment |
| **Educational supervisor** | "Portfolio helps trainees reflect more deeply, not less. The AI asks the questions you would ask." | Show follow-up question flow: "What was your differential?", "How did this change your practice?" | "This will make lazy trainees lazier" → AI scaffolds reflection, doesn't replace it |

### Messaging Principles

1. **Never say "writes your portfolio for you"** — this triggers probity concerns. Say "helps you structure your reflections" or "drafts from your clinical experience"
2. **Lead with time saved, not AI** — trainees care about getting their evenings back, not the technology
3. **ARCP is the emotional driver** — frame everything around passing ARCP with confidence and reducing pre-ARCP anxiety
4. **Privacy-first language** — proactively address data safety in all messaging. "Your data stays yours" is not a footnote, it's a headline
5. **Complement, don't compete with FourteenFish** — "Portfolio helps you create better entries. FourteenFish is where they live." Never position against the mandated platform
6. **Cite RCGP guidance directly** — "The RCGP states it 'does not believe it is possible to mandate that GPs in training should never use AI when completing learning logs or trainee portfolios.'" This legitimises your product with the authority trainees trust

### Core GTM Motion

**Product-led, community-seeded, bottom-up adoption.**

This is not an enterprise sale. Individual trainees discover the product, try the free tier, experience the value, and tell their VTS group. The growth engine is:

1. Trainee discovers Portfolio (Reddit, peer recommendation, social media)
2. Signs up, creates first entry with voice → sees the magic moment (structured entry with capabilities mapped)
3. Checks coverage dashboard → sees gaps they didn't know about → emotional hook (ARCP anxiety)
4. Creates 3+ entries → hits free tier limit → converts to Pro
5. Tells their VTS WhatsApp group → 3-5 peers sign up → cycle repeats

## Pricing

| Tier | Price | Includes | Purpose |
|------|-------|---------|---------|
| **Free** | £0 | 3 entries/month, text input only, coverage dashboard, PDP tracking | Remove all friction to trial. Let users experience the magic moment (structured entry + capability mapping) |
| **Pro** | £7.99/month or £59.99/year | Unlimited entries, voice input, priority AI processing, data export, full version history | Core revenue. Annual pricing aligns with training year (August-August) |

### Pricing Rationale

- **Freemium is essential** — medical trainees are price-sensitive (junior doctor salary ~£32-40k). Need to prove value before asking for money
- **3 entries/month on free** — enough to experience the value (one entry proves the workflow), not enough for a full portfolio (trainees need 2-4 entries/week during active rotations)
- **Voice input as Pro feature** — this is the primary differentiator and the "wow" moment. Free users can type; Pro users can talk. Creates clear upgrade motivation
- **£59.99/year** — less than one locum shift (~£400-800/day). Compared to FourteenFish at £321/year (£963/3yr), this is positioned as "cheaper than what you already pay, and actually helpful"
- **Annual billing default** — reduces churn, aligns with the training year, and improves LTV. Monthly option exists for those who want flexibility
